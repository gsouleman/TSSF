Hospital Management System

HMS — Product demo

A concise walkthrough for stakeholders: what the platform does, who it serves, and how the main workflows connect.

Tip: Use → / ← or click to advance (see bottom bar).

What is HMS?

  • Web-based hospital operations software (PHP + MySQL/MariaDB).
  • Supports multiple facilities (sites) with scoped data and switching.
  • Covers front office, clinical documentation, labs & imaging, pharmacy, billing, insurance, inventory, and reporting.

Who is it for?

  • Clinical: doctors, nurses — chart, vitals, consultations.
  • Diagnostics: laboratory & radiology — orders, results, PDF reports.
  • Revenue cycle: cashier, billing, insurance configuration.
  • Patients: optional portal for appointments and result notices.
  • Administration: staff, facilities, audit, security.

Architecture in one slide

  • Presentation: PHP pages + shared layout (header/footer).
  • Domain logic: reusable modules under includes/.
  • Data: relational schema evolved via SQL migrations.
  • Security: sessions, CSRF tokens, role/ACL checks, audit log.
  • PDFs: receipts, clinical results, inventory reports (Dompdf).

Patient journey (happy path)

  1. Register / find patient — Patients.
  2. OPD queue & triage — vitals optional.
  3. Consultation — chief complaint, prescribed lab/radiology lines.
  4. Cashier — payment code, partial pay, receipts.
  5. Lab / radiology — verify code, capture results, finalize.
  6. Pharmacy — dispense; stock may link to inventory.

Clinical chart & documentation

  • Allergies, medications, vitals, consultations summary.
  • Prescribed tests and linked results when available.
  • Primary insurance % for patient co-pay at cashier.
  • External documents (outside lab/imaging) uploaded for continuity of care.

Billing & insurance

  • Service catalog and charges; receipts & company invoices.
  • Payment tickets bundle consultation + prescribed lines.
  • Insurer/carrier setup; patient policy and flexible insurer/patient split (0–100%).
  • Credit / receivables and expense register (when migrations applied).

Inventory & pharmacy

  • Stock items with automatic SKU, categories, reorder alerts.
  • Stock movements (receive, adjust, dispense from prescription).
  • Medical supply catalogue suggestions by category on add item.

Staff portals

  • Focused landing pages: doctors, nursing, laboratory, radiology, pharmacy, cashier, accountant, front desk.
  • Role-based navigation; ACL can refine permissions per deployment.

Integrations & APIs

  • REST-style JSON API (v1) for selected resources (auth, facilities, patients, appointments, …).
  • Sample FHIR Patient read for interoperability demos.

Demo script (5 minutes)

  1. Show dashboard / portal for your persona.
  2. Open a patient → clinical chart → consultation link.
  3. Show cashier payment code and a receipt PDF.
  4. Open lab or radiology portal → service code verify → result workflow.
  5. Optional: inventory low-stock alert and PDF catalog.
